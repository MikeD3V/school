ALL ABOUT MYSELF

My name is Althea Alvarado, a 17-year-old Senior High School student at Don Bosco in Cebu, currently taking the ICT programming track. I have always been passionate about creativity and self-expression, which led me to the world of cosplay. Designing and making my own costumes, along with doing hair and makeup, has allowed me to showcase my artistic side.

Apart from my studies, I have ventured into small businesses, including a mini eatery and a dessert business. My love for arts and crafts inspired me to create satin flower arrangements, which helped me develop essential skills in managing and growing a business. Balancing school, business, and personal life has taught me the importance of hard work and determination.

As I continue my journey, I'm excited to keep learning and growing in ICT while also pushing my creative boundaries. I'm determined to keep working hard, following my passions, and building a future that I can be proud of. This is just the beginning of my story, and I can't wait to see where life takes me next.
